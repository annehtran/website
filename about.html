Who am I

I'm a Data Analyst with over 7 years of experience. I started as Business Data Analyst and worked in Singapore and Canada. In 2025, I elevated my career by returning to school in the United States to pursue the Business Management Certificate at University of California San Diego.

Holding the PL-300 Microsoft Power BI and ASTQB Software Quality Assurance certifications, I am deeply passionate about transforming clean, quality-assured data into dashboards that inform, influence, and inspire action.

I'm grateful for all the experiences and memories that my Data Analyst journey has brought into my life.
